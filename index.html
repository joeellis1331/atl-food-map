Exploring Atlanta's Culinary Scene

Check out our reviews of Atlanta's culinary scene, from ethnic dinners, rooftop bars, and delectable sweets. All the way to boutique coffee shops and bakeries.

**Our reviews are by no means comprehensive. There are likely delicious items which we simply did not order! Additionally, we know it can be challenging to find inventive and flavorful plant-based options. Therefore in our exploration, we try to always order a plant-based item when available (sometimes there just isn't one!), and highlight these options in our review.**
